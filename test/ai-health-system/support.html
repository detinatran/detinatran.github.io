Help & Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Use "AI Diagnosis" to enter symptoms and get suggestions

The function uses questions to filter symptoms and suggestions based on the information you provide.

How accurate is the AI Diagnosis?

Our AI Diagnosis tool provides suggestions based on common patterns and medical knowledge. However, it is for informational purposes only and should not replace a consultation with a qualified healthcare professional. Always seek medical advice for any health concerns.

Is my data secure?

We take data privacy and security seriously. For logged-in users, your diagnosis history is stored securely. Please review our Privacy Policy for more details (link to be added).

Can I use this service as a guest?

Yes, you can use the AI Diagnosis feature as a guest. However, your diagnosis history will not be saved. Signing up allows for a personalized experience and history tracking.

What if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, you can use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset it. An email will be sent to your registered email address with instructions.
